CAMPUS LIFE

More than just knwoledge!

Your education goes well beyond your coursework. Extracurricular activities can form a vital part of your experience here at NSBM, creating unique opportunities for friendship and learning.Your activities complement your studies. Your commitment level is entirely up to you. life at NSBM is enriched with numerous events and cultural activities. This one-of-a-kind university is the place for all students to showcasw their skills in music, dance, art, and theatre. It also provides opportunities for students to get exposure to different ethnic, religious and cultural activities. “We Got Talent”, “Siyapathsiya Udanaya”, Christmas Carols, NSBM Saddharmaloka Vesak Zone, “Aloko Udapadi – Poson Bethi Gee Saraniya” and NSBM Walk are some of those exciting activities. The objective of organizing these events is to provide opportunities for students to develop and identify their skills and creativity while ensuring that NSBM Green University Town produces individuals who respect different cultures.
