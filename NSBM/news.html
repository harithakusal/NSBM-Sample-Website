Department of Technical Education and Training selects NSBM as a model project

Senior Management team and Heads of Collages of Department of Technical Education and Training (DTET) who are going through their Strategic Planning Process in collaboration with Colombo Plan Staff College (CPSC) in Manilla, Philippines under the theme "Organizational Strengthening through Strategic Direction" have selected NSBM Green University Town as a model project to study. The team lead by Dr. Chithal Ambawatte, Faculty Consultant of CPSC visited NSBM Grren University Town last week and studied NSBM model and processes in detail and gathered inputs for their planning process.

Poson at NSBM

Led by the Vice Chancellor Dr E.A. Weerasinghe and the Management, NSBM Green University was in the fore front of commemorating the advent of Buddhist Philosophy in Sri Lanka with the establishment of Buddha Sasana by Arahat Mahinda Thero, none other than the son of Emperor Dharmashoka in India. A comprehensive and meaningful Dhamma program was conducted day prior to Poson including Dhamma Sermon by Ven Mawarale Badhdhiya Thero for students as well as parents. The health camp including Eye, Dental and General clinic for NSBM neighboring community was attended by a large number from the community where apart from general health checks , distribution of Spectacles also took place. Over 500 people including members of security forces came forward to donate blood while the traditional dan sal were offering Ice cream, Kadala, Pol rotti, Soft drinks etc throughout the day. NSBM students and staff performed “Bhakthi Gee” in the evening thus depicting Buddhist art and culture through songs and dance forms. In order to commemorate this significant day where Sri Lankan Buddhists still cherish Buddhist philosophy , NSBM launched a video with a poson song produced within the expertise of NSBM for which lyrics was written by its consultant Bandula Nanayakkarawasam while the Music was produced by Anjana Ayeshan and Yashod Gamage with the vocals from - Hasala Ranathunga | Madhawa Samarasekara In the evening, the University was decorated by colorful Poson lanterns with a special display of “Mihinthalawa” and “Mihindu Anuruwa”. The donation for needy will take place in Homes for the age and Children and those affected the recent floods during the week. NSBM will continue with her efforts to retain Buddhist values and culture among the majority of the students and also respect and encourage practicing of other faiths within the University premises.

NSBM declares open its IT R&D and Business Incubation Center

Keeping in line with NSBM’s vision of producing globally employable graduates, NSBM’s IT R&D and Business Incubation Center was declared open last week under the distinguished patronage of Hon. Dr. Sarath Amunugama – Minister of Science, Technology, Research, Skills Development and Vocational Training & Upcountry Heritage at the Students Center of NSBM Green University Town, Pitipana, Homagama. At this phase, three companies namely 99X Technology Limited, Omobio Private Limited and Creative Technology Solutions Private Limited (Creative Software) have commenced operations and offered 30 full time internship opportunities to undergraduates of NSBM to undergo training within this NSBM IT R&D and Business Incubation Centre. Hon. Dr. Sarath Amunugama also participated in the event of the official exchange of MoUs with Mr. Anura Adikari – Senior Tech Lead & University Relationship Manager of 99X Technology, Mr. Sushena Ranathuna – CEO of Creative Software, and Mr. Eranga Weeraratne - CEO of Omobio, which was followed by the handover of letters of appointment to the NSBM undergraduates who have attained internships with the aforementioned companies. NSBM’s ‘Industry Partnership Program’(IPP) is designed to optimize synergies among the Industry Partners and NSBM in relation to Entry Level Talent Acquisition (Internship / Training), Employer Brand Building, Knowledge Sharing, Corporate Brand Building, Special Projects, Incubation / Entrepreneurship Development and Talent Acquisition of fresh graduates. All undergraduate programs of NSBM are designed to enable the students to gain the much-needed Industry Exposure, on a full-time basis, from the end of their 2nd year of study onwards, where lectures for 3rd and 4thyears are conducted only on weekends. This is a unique feature of NSBM which assures employability of graduates. NSBM Green University Town (www.nsbm.lk ) is a fully pledged University Town, built on a 26-acre premise at Homagama. It will record the highest student population among the state and private universities by the end of year 2018. NSBM offers UGC approved and foreign undergraduate and post-graduate programmes in three streams, namely; Business Management, Computing and Engineering. Mrs. Sandya Wijebandara - Secretary of the Science, Technology, Research, Skills Development and Vocational Training and Upcountry Heritage, Mr. P. Ranepura - Chairman of NSBM, Dr. E. A. Weerasinghe – Vice Chancellor of NSBM, Mr. Chaminda Rathnayake - Deputy Vice Chancellor of NSBM, Mr. Chaminda Wijesinghe, Dean – School of Computing, Academic and Non-academic members of NSBM and other distinguished invitees were among the participants

His Excellency Archbishop Pierre Nguyen Van Tot Apostolic Nuncio visit

His Excellency Archbishop Pierre Nguyen Van Tot Apostolic Nuncio (Ambassador of the Vatican) visited NSBM Green University on 21st June 2018. Apostolic Nuncio is the official representative Pope Francis in Sri Lanka. In fact, it was an important day for Catholic and Christian student community of the university as this is the first such visit by a distinguished member of Catholic Church hierarchy. The Deputy Vice Chancellor Mr. Chaminda Rathnayake, Senior Manager Mr. Raja Premadasa, academic and non-academic staff members of NSBM warmly received the Rev. Van Tot and the catholic dignitaries to the NSBM Green University Town. Rev. Van Tot also celebrated a holy mass with Catholic and Christian Students of the NSBM Green University town. Students also visited NSBM to witness this momentous day at the NSBM Green University Town. Archbishop Van Tot was highly impressed about the NSBM university and its operations. He expressed his sincere blessings for the future success of the organization. Students of Catholic and Christian Students Society of NSBM organized the event under the guidance of Vice Chancellor of NSBM Dr. E. A Weerasinghe and academic staff.
